Hi, i'm kingsley

My name is Kingsley Anyabuike Chubie. I was born October 11th 1997 in Lagos Nigeria (20 years old), I am currently based in Lagos Nigeria, I have a younger sister (Kimberly Anyabuike Chiwendu), and I am a graduate of Computer Science from Babcock University (2018). I am the C.E.O of Kintal Software a small startup Solutions company and we are currently building our first product.

I am a self-taught artist (trditional and digital), 2D animator, web designer and developer, UI/UX designer, writer and mobile app developer. I have worked on different projects across these fields, group and personal.

Art

I do both Tradiational and Digital Art. I have been doing Tradiational art for as long as i can remember, but I started digital art when I got my first computer (at age 8). I used MS Paint for a very long time, until 2016 when I decided to try an actual drawing software. So, I currently draw and design with Manga Studio (now called CLIP Studio).

Intially, I was into comic art, and I wrote and drew a few comics, none of which are actually published, but can be found on my facebook page. I still plan to go back to comic making much later.

I cuttently just do regular drawings, concept art for the aformentioned future comics, fan art etc. All of which can be seen here and on my Personal Instagram Page.

Animation

I got into 2d animation around the same time I started digital art. I originally made my Animations with MS paint and Windows Movie Maker (which aren't animation tools). I made them by drawing the frames, saving them as pictures, and compiling them on Windows Movie Maker. The very first full Animation I made was a trailer for an old comic I used to make (called Gonzo). I posted it on youtube in 2013.

It wasn't until after High School I decided to switch to Adobe Flash (Now called Animate). Which greatly improved the quality of my Animations.

I currently make short animated skits on my Instagram Page. I don't do commisssions or paid jobs, as animating is simply just a hobby to me.

Web Deisign/Development

I first got into Web development during my final year of high school. I wanted to build a website, but didn't know anything about programming (I just thought it was like a drag'n'drop thing XD). So I bought Adobe Dreamweaver and strated learning, slowly but gradually. I became good at HTML and CSS by the end of my second year in University.

I now do fullstack web design and development, I am currently building the web platform (frontend and backend) for a startup app called Vybite (coming soon). I have also worked on a number of sites like this one and web apps for school projects hosted on my Github Page.

I am highly proficient in frontend and backend web development languages: HTML5, CSS 4, JavaScript, PHP and Node.js as well as variouds Libraries: React.js, jquery, Vue.js and Express.js

App Development

I must admit, i'm still new to mobile app development. Also, I do hybrid and not native app development. Although I have been familiarized with native Android app development (Java and XML). I also haven't built a complete mobile app as of yet.

However, I am currently building a mobile app for the startup previously mentioned above. I build mobile apps with a hybrid JavaScript framework called React Native.

Apart from React Native, I recently learnt Flutter and may be using it for future projects, as it is still in early stages of development (beta).
