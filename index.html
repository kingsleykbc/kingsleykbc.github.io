What is Vybite ?

Vybite is an event recommendation and Ticketing platform. Different events, movies, parties, live shows etc. get recommended to the users in a seamless easy-to-use app. While the hosts and companies who create these events are provided with a special application to track and monitor sales, view customer analytics and much more.

Unique features of Vybite over other event and ticketing applications

Vybite for Customers

A mobile app available on Android and iOS. The customers are the main users of vybite, and the app allows them browse events, get event recommendations, but and/or transfer tickets, receive invites for private events and ceremonies, and much more.

Using Vybite to purchase tickets to some of your favorite movies, shows, games etc. is cheaper, more convenient and a lot less stressful. See more benefits below.

Vybite for Hosts

A web application for individuals and companies who host these events. It is flexible i.e. suitable for all kinds of events, from single date private parties hosted by individuals to recurring movies hosted by cinemas. Aside from hosting and managing events, Vybite lets hosts track daily ticket sales, view statistics of the customers such as average age demographic and gender to help with marketing of future events.

Using Vybite to host your events, is cheap, reliable, more effective, and would help you reach more people, especially the ones who are most likely to purchase your tickets.

Why use Vybite ?

You might be wondering why you should use vybite. Maybe you don't attend events often, or you probably already use other services. Here are a few reasons why you should use the Vybite platform:

  • Simplicity: Vybite is easy to use. The entire process involves, finding an event you like, viewing the available tickets, and tapping the pay button.
  • Affordabilty: Low on cash? not a problem. you can find free events happening around you. You can also take advantage of ticket perks, that come with discounts.
  • Time saving: Buying an e-ticket on Vybite is a lot less time-consuming than waiting in line, or buying from third party vendors. Also, you can buy tickets as soon as the event is posted, so you don't miss out on sold out events.
  • Value: Vybite allows reviews of events and hosts. So before buying a ticket for an ongoing, you can check reviews, to be sure the ticket is worth buying.
  • Rewards: Vybite also has perks attached to some tickets. You can claim these perks when the ticket you buy fufills one or more conditions stated by the host. These perks include discounts, and other rewards specified by the host.
