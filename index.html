So, what is Vybite ?

Vybite is an event hosting, recommendation and ticketing application.
It is a platform where individuals and companies can host their movies, parties, live shows, seminars etc. and have them recommended to the right group of people / customers, while the customers can buy, send and find tickets to some of their favorite events.

As a User...

With Vybite, you can find and buy tickets to all kinds of events you might be interested in. Awesome Movies, trending shows, cool parties even private gatherings being hosted by friends and family.
Cool events you might be interested in will be recommended to you and you can even search based on your mood.
Low on cash? not a problem as you can also find lot's of free events.
You can also take advantage of some amazing perks and discounts that could be attached to some tickets.
The app is very simple and ticket buying is super easy with just a few clicks. All you need to do is find an event, pick the date and time and pay (with the option to call an Uber or get directions to the venue). No need to check emails, verify account or put in credit card details everytime you want to pay.

As an event host...

As an individual or company that want's to host an event (either one time or ocassionally), Vybite is for you. You can create single or multiple date events (movies, parties, seminars, party) etc.
Vybite provides you with a separate interactive system to properly manage your event(s) i.e tracking ticket sales, viewing revenue, viewing customers reviews, getting event statisics like average customer age, location, gender etc. to better help you in marketing.
When you host your event on vybite. I gets recommended to people who are most likely to be interested in it. Therefore increasing yout chances of selling out tickets.
The event hosting system is very flexible, and tailored to fit all kinds of events; single/multiple dates, single/multiple ticket types, seat allocation, ticket design etc.
With Vybite, you can save money on heavy advertising, selling tickets physically or through 3rd party vendors and generate more money by having to tickets marketed to the right customers.

Not Convinced Yet ?

If you aren't still sure weather or not Vybite is for you, Here are some unique features Vybite has over other Events and Ticketing applications.

  • Vybite covers ALL Kinds of events including Movies, Private Gatherings, Meetups etc.
  • Perks: Event hosts can can place certain rewards and discounts on their event tickets.
  • Mood Search: Users have the ability to search for events based on their current mood. e.g Fun, Romantic, Tourist, Bored, "Low on Cash", Adrenaline etc.
  • Hosting of Private events, with invitations sent out to specific users.
  • You can review events you attend, and also see reviews and comments of ongoing events.
  • Uber Integration: You can call an Uber from the app before or after buying your ticket to the event.
